# STEP 14 — Understand Support

Support tells how frequently a rule appears.

Support(A \Rightarrow B)=\frac{Count(A\cap B)}{Total\ Transactions}

Example:
If 5 out of 20 records satisfy rule:

```text id="vtghpi"
Support = 5/20 = 25%
```

---

# STEP 15 — Understand Confidence

Confidence tells accuracy of rule.

Confidence(A \Rightarrow B)=\frac{Count(A\cap B)}{Count(A)}

Example:
If 4 out of 5 Finance employees have High performance:

```text id="wd93ij"
Confidence = 4/5 = 80%
```

# VIVA QUESTIONS

## What is preprocessing?

Cleaning and transforming data before mining.

---

## What is discretization?

Conversion of numeric data into intervals/bins.

---

## Why use Apriori?

To discover frequent patterns and relationships.

---

## What is support?

Frequency of occurrence of a rule.

---

## What is confidence?

Accuracy/reliability of a rule.

---

## Difference between classification and association mining?

| Classification | Association Mining  |
| -------------- | ------------------- |
| Predicts class | Finds relationships |
| Supervised     | Unsupervised        |
